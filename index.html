Introduction

DJS Racing is proud to continue our successful partnership with JLCPCB, a cornerstone of our high-performance electric vehicle endeavors.

Once again, JLCPCB has been instrumental in our progress, providing us with exceptional multi-layer PCBs that are the foundation of our high-voltage battery pack. Their reliable, long-lasting components have been essential for our intricate circuit designs.

We've made significant strides this year, including the development of our in-house Battery Management System (BMS) PCBs, made possible through JLCPCB's continued support. Their cost-effective solutions have been invaluable in optimizing our budget and allowing us to invest in other critical project areas.

JLCPCB's rapid turnaround times and dedicated support team remain essential to our efficiency. We're excited to expand our collaboration by integrating their PCBs into our new data acquisition system, a crucial step in refining our vehicle's performance.

To further enhance our vehicle's capabilities, we're incorporating an Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) using JLCPCB's trusted PCBs. This will provide invaluable data for optimizing our vehicle's design.

We look forward to the continued growth of our partnership with JLCPCB as we push the boundaries of electric vehicle technology.

Supplies and Testing Steps

Step 1: Continuity Test - A continuity test ensures proper connectivity of conductive traces on the PCB.

Step 2: Power Up Unsoldered PCB - Check for short circuits or electrical faults before soldering.

Step 3: Soldering and Testing - Gradually solder components and perform incremental testing.

Step 4: Complete PCB Testing - Comprehensive testing under controlled lab conditions.

Step 5: Real-World Testing - Test PCBs in the vehicle to ensure proper functionality under real conditions.

Our project has successfully completed one iteration of the first four steps, and we are excited to further test, validate, and recalibrate a fresh batch of high-quality PCBs by JLCPCB to lock in our design.
